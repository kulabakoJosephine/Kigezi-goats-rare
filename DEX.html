Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before placing your order.

How do I pay for my goats?
We accept Mobile Money (MTN & Airtel), cash on collection, and bank transfer. For deliveries, payment is confirmed before dispatch. Contact us on WhatsApp to arrange payment.
Do you offer delivery? How far?
Yes! We deliver within Kabale district and nearby areas including Kisoro and Rukungiri. Delivery cost depends on distance and number of goats. WhatsApp us for a delivery quote before ordering.
Are the goats vaccinated and dewormed?
Yes, all our goats are fully vaccinated and dewormed before sale. We also provide a basic health record card with each purchase so you know exactly what treatment has been given.
What breeds do you keep?
We specialize in local Kigezi goat breeds, well-adapted to the highland climate of Kabale. These breeds are hardy, fast-growing, and thrive on local pasture — ideal for both meat and dairy.
Can I visit the farm before buying?
Absolutely! We welcome farm visits daily from 10am to 7pm. Located in Katuna Kigezi region, Kabale district. You can see and choose your goats in person. Call or WhatsApp us before visiting so we can prepare for you.
Do you offer after-sale support?
Yes! Our on-site animal clinic team is available for follow-up questions. We also sell all the feed and supplements your new goats need. We want your goats to thrive, so we support you beyond the sale.

Our Story

About Kigezi Goats Rare

Nestled in the lush hills of Kabale district in Katuna, Kigezi region, our farm is a haven for local goat breeds raised with care, purpose, and tradition. We specialize in both meat and breeding goats, offering affordable prices to support local farmers and families.

Beyond livestock, we run a fully equipped animal clinic and a stocked feed store — ensuring your goats thrive from birth to market. We're open daily from 10am to 7pm, welcoming you with expertise and heart.

Whether you're starting your first herd or expanding an existing one, Kigezi Goats Rare is your trusted partner in sustainable animal management.
