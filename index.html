About Kigezi Goats Rare

Nestled in the lush hills of Kabale district in Katuna,Kigezi region, our farm is a haven for local goat breeds raised with care, purpose, and tradition. We specialize in both meat and raring goats, offering affordable prices to support local farmers and families.

Beyond livestock, we run an animal clinic and a fully stocked feed store to ensure proper care and growth of your animals and ensuring your goats thrive from birth to market. We’re open daily from 10am to 7pm, welcoming you with expertise and heart.

Whether you're starting a herd or stocking your kitchen, Kigezi Goats rare is your trusted partner in sustainable animal management.
